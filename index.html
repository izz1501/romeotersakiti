Derivatives & Critical Points

Total Weight Model: $f(t)$
W(t) = a · exp(-b · exp(-k · t))
First Derivative (ADG): $f'(t)$
W'(t) = W(t) · b · k · exp(-k · t)
Second Derivative: $f''(t)$ Inflection Point Solver
W''(t) = W'(t) · k · (b · exp(-k · t) - 1)
When W''(t) = 0, the animal reaches its maximum physical growth rate (Day 251).
